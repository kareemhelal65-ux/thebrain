01

Stacked Wordmark

01 / 04 The Brain · AIOS Heavy condensed stack — top line in Paper, AIOS in Signal
Light — Paper #F2F2F0
Dark — Void #0B0B0D
Concept
Two-line stacked lockup. THE BRAIN in heavy condensed above, AIOS in Signal below — identical weight, identical size. Structural top and bottom rules frame it as a stamped block.
Strengths
Scales from small nav to billboard. Clean brand split: identity above, product category below. Signal on second line follows the 70/25/5 rule.
Watch
Horizontal span is wide. Use stacked (square) orientation for tight spaces.

Stamp Lockup

03 / 04 The Brain · AIOS Stamp Square stamp format — framed with top/bottom rules + crop ticks
Light
Dark
Concept
Square framed stamp: outer border, top/bottom horizontal rules, THE · BRAIN set large centre, AIOS in Signal below. Signal crop ticks at all four corners.
Strengths
Dense editorial presence. Works as a social avatar, packaging mark, or keynote title card. Carries the full visual grammar in one frame. Square format suits favicon use.
Watch
Not suited for small UI nav — use the horizontal lockup there. This format is for brand-first contexts.
04

Signal-Inverse Horizontal

04 / 04 Signal Fill · Inverse Wordmark Signal orange fill bar behind THE BRAIN, AIOS in Void below — high-energy variant
Light
Dark
Concept
Signal fills the top block, framing THE BRAIN in Void text — a direct inversion of the standard palette. AIOS sits below the rule in Void on Paper.
Strengths
Highest immediate impact. Announces the brand. Good for hero sections, slide covers, and email headers where one dominant Signal block is appropriate.
Watch
Uses Signal as background — technically violates the 5% signal rule for continuous use. Reserved for single-use hero moments only, not nav or repeated UI surfaces.
Recommendation
Primary: Concept 02 — Mark + Horizontal Wordmark. The 3×3 grid mark is the strongest standalone symbol: font-independent, favicon-ready, and directly grounded in the brand grammar. The horizontal lockup with mono tagline handles every nav and header context cleanly.

Secondary: Concept 01 — Stacked Wordmark for tight-width or stacked contexts (mobile nav, sidebar header, email).

Hero/editorial: Concept 03 — Stamp Lockup for social avatars, slide covers, and brand-first placement.

Concept 04 is a reserved accent — use only for single high-impact hero moments, not recurring surfaces.
